Adrian Sims

Adrian Sims a native of Atlanta, Ga was born in Louisville, Kentucky. Adrian studied Music and Business Administration at the University of Louisville. While in Louisville, Ky, Adrian managed a team of 18 Contact Center Agents for Humana Healthcare. He also managed a team of 22 Contact Center agents for Accent Warranty. Adrian relocated to Atlanta, GA as Contact Center Director managing a team 34 employees for Sears Home Central. It was during late 90’s that the dotcom boom was evolving quickly and Adrian’s passion for technology blossomed.

Adrian transitioned from Contact Center Management to Technology Sales and Business Development. A consummate professional, Adrian was very successful in Technology Sales and a great proponent for business solutions that streamlined business processes through technology. Adrian is a quick study, and will work hard to understand how a business solution can help his clients. It with this attitude that Adrian is knowledgeable multiple business applications.

Adrian’s confidence, smile, and charming personality has allowed him to build great relationships in the community. The list of technology solutions that Adrian has knowledge of are as follows, Cloud Storage, VoIP, MPLS, Fiber, Metro Ethernet, Cisco Unified Communications Software and Hardware. Adrian also has knowledge of IT Network Security, EDI Secure Connectivity software, Financial Accounting, Expense Management, Asset Management, Human Capital Management, Supply Chain Management, Transportation Management, Business Intelligence, Performance Management, Enterprise Architecture and data modeling.Adrian is poised to becoming a driving force is development with new and bright ideas. The sky is the limit to what came be achieved through hard work and dedication.
